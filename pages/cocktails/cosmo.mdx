## Steps

<Steps>

### Fill serving glasses with ice and citrus peel.

Peel about 5 inches of any citrus peel and place twist on top of ice. The peel adds a nice aroma to the drink.

### Combine other ingredients in a shaker with ice.

### Shake vigorously.

### Strain into serving glasses.
