## Cold Brew Filters

I just search "Cold Brew Filters" on Amazon and buy what I think looks reasonable. [This](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B09VL5CK5J) is my most recent purchase, its a little small but it works for this recipe.

## Steps

<Steps>

### Fill pitcher with water Fill your pitcher with cold, filtered water.

I use a 2 quart pitcher so I can water the coffee down later.

### Fill bag with coffee grounds.

### Place bag in pitcher.

### Set in fridge for 8-24 hours.

### (Optional) water down coffee to taste.

### Serve over ice.

With optional milk and/or syrup.

</Steps>
