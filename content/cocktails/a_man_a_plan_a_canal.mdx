# 🍸 A Man A Plan A Canal

A tropical tiki-style cocktail with layers of rum, banana liqueur, and fresh citrus.

## Steps

<Steps>

### Build in shaker

Add pineapple juice, lime juice, orange juice, simple syrup, banana liqueur, dark rum, and overproof rum to a shaker.

### Add ice and shake

Fill with ice and shake well.

### Open pour into mug

Pour the entire contents (ice included) into a tiki mug or rocks glass.

### Garnish with fresh mint

Slap a sprig of fresh mint to release the oils and place it on top.

</Steps>
